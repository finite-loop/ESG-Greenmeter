**Background on GRI Requirements:**

The GRI Standards 2021 framework is organized as:

**Universal Standards:**
- GRI 2: General Disclosures — organizational details, governance, strategy, stakeholder engagement

**Economic Standards:**
- GRI 201: Economic Performance — direct economic value generated/distributed, climate financial risks
- GRI 202: Market Presence — entry-level wage ratios, local hiring
- GRI 203: Indirect Economic Impacts — community investment, infrastructure contributions
- GRI 204: Procurement Practices — local supplier spending
- GRI 205: Anti-corruption — training coverage, confirmed incidents
- GRI 206: Anti-competitive Behavior — legal actions

**Environmental Standards:**
- GRI 301: Materials — renewable/non-renewable materials used, recycled inputs
- GRI 302: Energy — total consumption, electricity, renewable share, intensity, reductions
- GRI 303: Water and Effluents — withdrawal, consumption, discharge, recycling, stress areas
- GRI 304: Biodiversity — sites in/near protected areas, habitat impacts
- GRI 305: Emissions — Scope 1, 2, 3 GHG, intensity, reductions, air pollutants
- GRI 306: Waste — generation, diversion from disposal, hazardous waste, landfill

**Social Standards:**
- GRI 401: Employment — new hires, turnover, benefits, parental leave
- GRI 402: Labor/Management Relations — notice periods for operational changes
- GRI 403: Occupational Health & Safety — TRIR, LTIFR, fatalities, absenteeism
- GRI 404: Training and Education — average hours, performance reviews, investment
- GRI 405: Diversity and Equal Opportunity — women in management/board, gender pay gap
- GRI 406: Non-discrimination — incidents reported and resolved
- GRI 407: Freedom of Association — operations where rights are at risk
- GRI 408: Child Labor — incidents, operations at risk
- GRI 409: Forced or Compulsory Labor — incidents, operations at risk
- GRI 413: Local Communities — operations with community engagement
- GRI 414: Supplier Social Assessment — screening rates, improvements
- GRI 416: Customer Health and Safety — products assessed, safety incidents
- GRI 418: Customer Privacy — substantiated complaints, data breaches
