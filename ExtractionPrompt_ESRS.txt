**Background on ESRS Requirements:**

The ESRS framework (EFRAG Delegated Act, August 2023) mandates reporting across the following standards:

- ESRS 2: General Disclosures — governance, strategy, impact/risk/opportunity management, metrics & targets
- ESRS E1: Climate Change — GHG emissions (Scope 1, 2, 3), energy consumption, transition plans, carbon pricing
- ESRS E2: Pollution — air emissions (NOx, SOx, PM, VOCs), water pollution, soil contamination
- ESRS E3: Water and Marine Resources — water withdrawal, consumption, discharge, stress area exposure
- ESRS E4: Biodiversity and Ecosystems — land use, protected areas, species impacts
- ESRS E5: Resource Use and Circular Economy — waste generation, recycling rates, material inputs, circularity
- ESRS S1: Own Workforce — headcount, diversity, pay equity, health & safety, training, engagement
- ESRS S2: Workers in the Value Chain — supplier screening, social audits, corrective actions
- ESRS S3: Affected Communities — community engagement, grievance mechanisms
- ESRS S4: Consumers and End-Users — product safety, data privacy, recalls
- ESRS G1: Business Conduct — anti-corruption, lobbying, whistleblowing, tax transparency



**Your Task:**

Systematically extract all quantitative and qualitative ESG metrics from each document. These metrics typically include:

- Energy consumption (total, fossil, renewable) and intensity metrics
- GHG emissions (Scope 1, 2 location-based, 2 market-based, 3 by category)
- GHG intensity and reduction targets (vs base year, net-zero target)
- Air pollutant emissions (NOx, SOx, PM2.5, VOCs)
- Water withdrawal, consumption, discharge, recycling, and stress area metrics
- Waste generation (total, hazardous, non-hazardous), recycling rates, landfill diversion
- Biodiversity and land use metrics
- Circular economy and material input metrics
- Workforce statistics (headcount, diversity, permanent/temporary, non-employee workers)
- Health and safety (TRIR, LTIFR, fatalities, occupational disease rates)
- Remuneration (pay gaps, CEO-to-median ratio, living wage compliance)
- Training hours and investment
- Supply chain assessments and supplier sustainability scores
- Community engagement and grievance resolution
- Product safety, recalls, and data privacy incidents
- Anti-corruption training, incidents, fines
- Lobbying spend, tax paid, effective tax rate
- Whistleblower reports and resolution rates
- Internal carbon pricing
- Transition plan status and climate risk assessments
